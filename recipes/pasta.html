Pasta

Pasta is traditional dish from Italy - well, at least I think It is. I'm not entirely sure on that, but I do know it is delicious! Check out how to cook pasta below!

Ingredients

  • Choose your type of pasta. Examples include:
    • Rigatoni
    • Angel Hair - used often for Spaghetti!
    • Penne
  • Water. Just water.
  • Your favorite pot- not the green kind. IYKYK!

Procedure

  1. Fill your pot up with water and place it on a burner.
  2. Turn on the burner to high, and now wait for your water to boil. PRO TIP: Salt your water! it adds flavor to the pasta!
  3. Once your water is boiling (it should be bubbling) pour in your pasta
  4. As your pasta boils, periodically check if it's past the point of "sticky." You don't want your pasta to be too sticky nor too soggy.
  5. After roughtly 10 minutes, you should be ready to eat your pasta! PRO TIP: eat your pasta with red sauce or alfredo sauce to actually be able to enjoy it!

Important to note...

Your pasta should boil anywhere between 8-12 minutes. The time varies and is dependent on the pasta you are using. Anyways, enjoy your, uh, bland, plain pasta with no sauce...please tell me you used sauce...
